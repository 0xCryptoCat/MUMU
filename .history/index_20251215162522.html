THE ORIGIN

An OG Meme, Like Pepe

Born on 4chan's /biz/ in 2018, MuMu represents the eternal bullish spirit of the crypto community.

The Bull Market Mascot

MuMu (or Moomoo) emerged from 4chan's Business and Finance board as the personification of bullish market sentiment. Just like Pepe the Frog and Wojak, MuMu is an organic internet creation, not owned or controlled by anyone.

Taking inspiration from Apu Apustaja, MuMu stands in eternal competition with Bobo the Bear, representing the never-ending battle between bulls and bears.

Unique LP Burn Mechanism

No transfer tax. No wallet tax. Burns happen directly from the liquidity pool.

1

Trigger the Burn

Anyone can trigger a burn by sending 1 token to the LP address

2

LP Burns ~2.5%

The contract removes MUMU from the pool and sends it to the dead address

3

Price Goes Up

Same ETH, fewer tokens. The pool resyncs and price adjusts upward

Your tokens are never touched. Only LP tokens are burned. Every burn is on-chain and verifiable.
